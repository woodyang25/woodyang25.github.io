Short Bio

    Dr. Xinxin Fan is an associate professor in Institute of Computing Technology (ICT),   Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS). He is broadly interested in Distributed Security Algorithm. His current research interests are centered on secure Machine Learning, Decentralized Trust Computing, Edge Computing. He has published more than 40 paper at several top conferences and journals including KDD, CIKM, WWW, ICWS, CSUR, TPDS, TWEB, etc. Also, he is a recipient of Microsoft Award-Honorable Mention from ISCC 2018, and best paper runner up award from IPCCC 2017 and KSEM 2018. He is an IEEE & ACM member.
